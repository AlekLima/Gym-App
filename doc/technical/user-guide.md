## Getting Started

### Account Creation
1. Click on "Sign Up" in the navigation bar
2. Fill in your email, full name, and create a password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Click "Create Account"
4. Check your email for a verification link (if email verification is enabled)
5. Log in with your credentials

### Logging In
1. Click on "Login" in the navigation bar
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Password Reset
1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for password reset instructions
4. Follow the link to set a new password

## User Authentication

### Profile Management
- Access your profile by clicking on your name/avatar in the top-right corner
- Update your personal information (name, email)
- Change your password
- Adjust notification preferences

### Security Features
- Password requirements: Minimum 8 characters, recommended mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
- Session timeout: Automatic logout after 30 minutes of inactivity
- Remember me option: Stay logged in on trusted devices

## Managing Workout Routines

### Creating a New Routine
1. Navigate to "Workouts" → "My Routines"
2. Click the "+ New Routine" button
3. Enter a name for your routine (e.g., "Upper Body Strength")
4. Optionally add a description
5. Choose whether to make it public (visible to other users) or keep it private
6. Click "Create Routine"

### Editing a Routine
1. Go to "My Routines"
2. Find the routine you want to edit
3. Click the edit (pencil) icon
4. Modify the name, description, or visibility settings
5. Click "Save Changes"

### Deleting a Routine
1. Go to "My Routines"
2. Find the routine you want to delete
3. Click the delete (trash) icon
4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

### Browsing Public Routines
1. Navigate to "Workouts" → "Public Routines"
2. Browse routines shared by other users
3. Filter by muscle group, difficulty, or equipment needed
4. Click on a routine to view details
5. Use "Add to My Routines" to save a copy to your account

## Tracking Workouts

### Starting a Workout Session
1. Go to "Workouts" → "Start Workout"
2. Select a routine from your list
3. Choose the date (defaults to today)
4. Click "Start Session"

### Logging Sets During Workout
For each exercise in your routine:
1. Enter the weight you're lifting
2. Enter the number of repetitions completed
3. Click "Add Set"
4. Repeat for additional sets of the same exercise
5. When finished with an exercise, click "Next Exercise"
6. Continue until all exercises are logged

### Completing a Workout
1. After logging all sets for all exercises, click "Finish Workout"
2. Optionally add notes about your workout (how you felt, adjustments made, etc.)
3. Review your workout summary
4. Click "Save Workout"

### Viewing Past Workouts
1. Go to "Workouts" → "Workout History"
2. Filter by date range, routine type, or specific exercises
3. Click on any workout to view detailed statistics
4. See volume (weight × reps) trends for each exercise

## Progress Tracking

### Recording Body Measurements
1. Navigate to "Progress" → "Measurements"
2. Click "+ New Measurement"
3. Select the type of measurement (weight, body fat percentage, chest, waist, etc.)
4. Enter the value and date
5. Click "Save"

### Viewing Progress Charts
1. Go to "Progress" → "Charts"
2. Select a metric to view (weight, strength on specific exercises, etc.)
3. Choose the time range (last 7 days, 30 days, 3 months, year-to-date, custom)
4. View your progress visualized in an easy-to-read chart
5. Hover over data points to see exact values and dates

### Setting Goals
1. Visit "Progress" → "Goals"
2. Click "+ New Goal"
3. Choose what you want to track (weight loss, muscle gain, strength increase)
4. Set your target value and deadline
5. Track your progress toward the goal on your dashboard

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
- **Can't log in**: Make sure you're using the correct email and password. Use "Forgot Password" if needed.
- **Workout not saving**: Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, contact support.
- **Charts not loading**: Ensure you have JavaScript enabled in your browser.
- **Slow performance**: Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.

### Getting Help

## Tips for Success

### Getting the Most Out of Gym App
1. **Be consistent**: Log every workout, even if it's just a quick session
2. **Track accurately**: Record weights and reps as precisely as possible
3. **Review regularly**: Check your progress charts weekly to see trends
4. **Set realistic goals**: Start small and build up gradually
5. **Use the community**: Share routines and get feedback from other users
6. **Mix it up**: Try different routines to prevent plateaus
7. **Listen to your body**: Use the notes feature to track how you feel during workouts

### Best Practices for Workout Tracking
- Always warm up before logging your first set
- Record rest times in the notes if they're important to your training
- Update your one-rep max periodically in the strength tracking section
- Take progress photos monthly and compare them in the progress section
- Celebrate milestones - the app will notify you when you reach goals!
